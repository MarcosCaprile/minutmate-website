Privacy Policy

How MinutMate handles information submitted through this website.

Overview

Protecting personal data matters to MinutMate. This policy explains what information may be collected through this website and how it is used.

Data collected

We only collect data that you actively provide, such as your name and email address when joining the waitlist.

Technical access data may also be processed automatically by the hosting provider, such as IP address, browser type, access time, and device information.

Purpose of processing

Personal data submitted through the waitlist is used to provide updates about StudyConnect, MinutMate, and related launch information.

Hosting

This website is hosted using GitHub Pages. Access to the website may result in the processing of technical connection data by GitHub as the hosting provider.

Third-party services

Form submissions are processed through Formspree. Information entered into the waitlist form may be transmitted to Formspree for the purpose of collecting and managing submissions.

Data retention

Personal data is stored only for as long as necessary to fulfill the intended purpose, unless a longer storage period is required by law.

Your rights

You may request information about your stored personal data, as well as correction, deletion, or restriction of processing, where legally applicable.
